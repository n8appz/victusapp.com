WHAT IT DOES

Log anything, your way

Type it, scan a barcode, or photograph a whole recipe — Victus reads the ingredients and builds the dish, per-serving macros included.

Every number, sourced

Other trackers hand you a number and ask for trust. Victus cites where its nutrition data came from, so you can check the work before you log it.

More than macros

Vitamins and minerals tracked against daily values, trends over weeks and months, and portions in real units — grams, ounces, or “one large egg.”

True Apple Health sync

Reads your activity, writes your nutrition — micronutrients included. A real two-way sync with the Health app.

Private by design

No accounts. No ads. No selling your data. Your food log lives on your iPhone — not on our servers. Read the privacy policy.
